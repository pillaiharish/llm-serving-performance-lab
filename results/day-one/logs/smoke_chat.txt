TTFT (Transfer Transfer Function) and ITL (Iterative Transfer Function) are both techniques used in machine learning to improve the performance of deep neural networks by reducing overfitting.

**TTFT:**
- **Purpose:** It's designed to reduce the impact of overfitting, which occurs when a model learns the training data too well, capturing noise and details that don't generalize well.
- **Implementation:** The idea is to replace the weights of a layer with those of another layer, effectively "transferring" information from one layer to another without directly modifying its parameters.
